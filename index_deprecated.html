A protocol for Decentralized, Community-Owned Delivery Services

Can you imagine a future where food delivery isn’t controlled by single profit-driven platforms, but powered by an ecosystem composed of community-owned platforms? In this ecosystem, workers gain agency, data become auditable and tools built by one can be shared to everyone within the ecosystem.

This is not just a shift in technology—it’s a transformation in who gets to build and benefit from the future of work.

Be part of the decentralized revolution. Join the growing number of communities.
